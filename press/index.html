Boilerplate

PlayHype Games is an independent iOS game studio making bright, snappy, one-more-run games for iPhone and iPad. Founded in 2026, the studio builds arcade titles that are easy to pick up and hard to put down — all with hand-tuned, procedural art and no clutter. Its games are free to play and monetized respectfully: there are no banner or interstitial ads, only optional rewarded video that players choose to watch, plus a one-time Remove Ads purchase. Ball Up and Swarmly are available now on the App Store, with Freedem Birdz coming soon.

Games at a glance
