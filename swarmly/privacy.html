Privacy Policy for Swarmly

Last updated: June 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Swarmly ("we", "us", or "the app") handles information when you use our mobile game.

Information We Collect

  • Game progress. Your level, coins, owned cosmetics, and settings are stored only on your device and are not transmitted to us.
  • Advertising identifiers. We use Google AdMob to show ads. AdMob may collect a device advertising identifier and related data to serve and measure ads. With your permission (via the App Tracking Transparency prompt) these may be used for personalized advertising; if you decline, you will see non-personalized ads.
  • Purchases. In-app purchases are processed by Apple. We do not receive or store your payment details.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each governed by its own privacy policy:

  • Google AdMob — https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Apple App Store / StoreKit — https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

Children's Privacy

Swarmly is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Choices

You can reset your advertising identifier or limit ad tracking at any time in your device's Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking. Purchasing the "Remove Ads" option stops ads from being shown.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at playhypegames@gmail.com.
